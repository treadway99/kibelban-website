About Kibelban

Why He Decided to Run

Kibelban decided to run for governor after years of dedicating himself to restoring peace and unity in Baringo, a region that has long struggled with banditry and conflict among communities. Through his commitment and leadership, he helped bring together leaders from the Pokot, Tugen, and Marakwet communities for peace talks, encouraging dialogue instead of violence.

His efforts created hope and strengthened relationships among the people, leading many residents to see him as a leader capable of bringing lasting change. Encouraged by the communities he served, Kibelban chose to run for governor so he could expand his peace-building efforts and work toward greater security, development, and unity for the entire county.
